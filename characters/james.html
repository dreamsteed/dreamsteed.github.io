 ‎ ‎‎ ‎ ‎‎ ‎ ‎An aspiring musician, who has adopted a reclusive lifestyle, finds solace in the indulgence of gothic mystique and macabre. With an unkempt fascination for Halloween and all things spooky,

‎ ‎ ‎‎ ‎ ‎‎ ‎ ‎James aspires to craft music that attains a grim yet whimsical tone. James is often found in his room making tunes on his computer, but often heads to the local cemetery when feeling inspired by an idea or melody.

‎ ‎ ‎‎ ‎ ‎‎ ‎ ‎Although he has lead a fairly mundane life, living on the outskirts of Los Angeles, California, with music being the crux for his mental fortitude, this mundanity is eventually broken by the events of The Dark Symphony.
